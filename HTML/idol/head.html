Description

Early life and Education

Wang Yibo was born on 5 August 1997 in Luoyang, Henan, China. He started dancing when he was young. During his second year in secondary school in 2011, Wang participated in IBD national dance competition and placed in the top sixteen in the hip-hop category, he then became a trainee of Yuehua Entertainment.Prior to his debut, Wang trained in South Korea and studied in Hanlim Multi Art School.

Career

Wang Yibo debuted in 2014 as a member of the boy group Uniq and later began acting in films and dramas.He gained major popularity in 2019 through the drama series The Untamed, where he played Lan Wangji.Besides acting, he became a successful solo singer and dance mentor on shows such as Produce 101 and Street Dance of China.From 2020 to 2024, he starred in hit dramas and films including Legend of Fei, Hidden Blade, and War of Faith.He remains one of China's most popular entertainers, continuing to release music, act in films, and appear at major national events.
